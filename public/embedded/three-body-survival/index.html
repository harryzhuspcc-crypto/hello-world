What “three-body” means here
Real idea

In the three-body problem, three massive bodies all pull on each other. Unlike a simple two-body orbit, the long-term motion can become hard to predict.

How this game uses it

Instead of simulating exact astrophysics, the game turns that instability into readable survival weather: Stable windows, chaotic transitions, scorching alignments, and dark freeze collapses.

What to watch

If the sky grows hotter, get behind cover. If the light drops and the cold rushes in, get inside a house or shelter pod.
